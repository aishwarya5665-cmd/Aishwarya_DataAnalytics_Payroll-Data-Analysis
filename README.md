**PAYROLL DATA ANALYSIS DASHBOARD**
**PROJECT OVERVIEW**
The Payroll Data Analysis Dashboard project focuses on transforming raw payroll data into meaningful insights and interactive dashboards.
The objective is to help management monitor payroll expenses, understand department-wise salary distribution, track overtime and leave patterns, and support data-driven workforce planning.
This project converts complex payroll records into easy-to-understand visual analytics for decision-makers.
**BUSINESS OBJECTIVES**
Analyze monthly payroll costs and identify spending trends
Understand department-wise salary distribution
Monitor overtime trends and workload pressure
Study employee leave patterns for better resource planning
Build an interactive dashboard for management-level reporting
**DATASET DESCRIPTION**
Payroll Dataset (Sample Size:1250 records)
**Key Columns:**
Employee ID
Department
Salary
Pay Date
Overtime Hours
Leave Days
Employee Status (Active/Inactive)
The dataset represents employee payroll transactions across multiple months and departments.
**TOOLS AND TECHNOLOGIES**
SQL – Data extraction, aggregation, and KPI calculations
Excel / Google Sheets – Initial data review and validation
Power BI / Tableau – Data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard creation
Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
**DASHBOARD COMPONENTS**
**KPI Cards**
Total Employees
Monthly Payroll Cost
Average Salary
Average Overtime Hours
**Visualizations**
Line Chart – Monthly Payroll Cost Trend
Bar Chart – Salary Distribution by Department
Column Chart – Overtime Hours Trend
Table – Employee-level Payroll Details
**Filters & Slicers**
Department
Month / Year
Employee Status
**KEY INSIGHTS**
Monthly payroll cost shows a steady increasing trend, indicating workforce growth and salary increments.
IT and Operations departments contribute the highest share of payroll expenses.
Overtime hours peak during month-end and high-demand periods, highlighting workload pressure.
Leave patterns show seasonal trends, allowing predictable workforce planning.
A small group of employees accounts for a large portion of overtime, indicating possible resource imbalance.
**BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS**
Optimize overtime by redistributing workloads or hiring temporary staff during peak periods.
Conduct salary audits for high-cost departments to improve cost efficiency.
Use historical leave data to plan backup resources in advance.
Regularly monitor payroll dashboards to control costs and improve productivity.
Reduce dependency on overtime to improve employee well-being and retention.
**CONCLUSION**
The Payroll Data Analysis Dashboard provides clear visibility into payroll expenses, workforce behavior, and operational efficiency.
By leveraging interactive dashboards, management can make informed decisions to control costs, improve staffing strategies, and enhance organizational performance.
